Pepper Relish
 
From:  Gertie, 1942
 
 
4    Green peppers
4    Red peppers
4    Yellow peppers
7    Small onions
1 1/2     Pint vinegar
1 1/2    teaspoons salt (coarse)
2    Cup sugar
    
    
 
Grind peppers and onions.  Pour boiling water over them and let stand 15 minutes.  Strain, add sugar, salt, vinegar.  Boil 20 minutes and seal.  
Makes 4 pints.
