Lima-Broccoli Casserole
 
 
1        Package frozen broccoli
1        Package frozen lima beans
1        Can mushroom soup
1        Cup sour cream
1        Cup sliced water chestnuts
1        Package onion soup (dry)
3        Cups crispy rice cereal
3/4     Stick oleo
 
 
Undercook broccoli and limas, drain.  Blend soup and sour cream.  Add water and chestnuts and onion soup.  Brown cereal with oleo.  Top vegetable mixture with cereal.  Bake 350° for 30 minutes.
