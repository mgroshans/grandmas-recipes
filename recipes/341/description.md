Fish in Foil
 
 
Preheat oven to 450°. 
Place fish* on piece of heavy duty foil.  Salt, peppr, paprika to taste.  
Cover with vegetables of choice (i.e., onion, celery, carrot, pepper, celery, cabbage, etc.).
Fold in edges of foil to secure.  Bake approximately 15 minutes.
 
*Scrod / Snapper / Orange Roughie
