Creamy Onion Sauce
 
From:  Newspaper
 
 
1    Cup sour cream
1    Cup mayonnaise
1/4    Cup minced celery
2    Tablespoons minced onion
2    teaspoons curry powder
    
    
    
1.    In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients; mix well.
2.    Cover; chill until ready to serve.  Makes about 2 1/4 cups.
