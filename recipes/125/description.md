Carrot Cake
 
From:  Helen Earl
 
 
2    Cups grated carrots
1    Small can crushed pineapple (drain most juice)
1    teaspoon vanilla
1    teaspoon salt
1/2    teaspoon allspice
2    teaspoon cinnamon
1    Cup oil
3    Eggs
2    teaspoon baking soda
1    Cup nutmeats
2    Cups sugar
2    Cups sifted flour
    
 
Mix ingredients together.
Bake 350° for one hour.
