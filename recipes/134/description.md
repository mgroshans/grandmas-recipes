Fruit Cocktail Cake
 
From:  Mrs. Ream (1957)
 
 
1    Egg, well beaten
1    teaspoon soda
1    Cup sugar
1/2    teaspoon salt
1 1/2    Cups flour
2    Cups fruit cocktail
1    Cup brown sugar
1/2    Cup chopped nuts
 
 
Mix soda, sugar, salt and flour.  Then add egg and fruit cocktail.  Pour into greased pan.  Top with brown sugar and nuts.  Pat down and bake 325° for 45 minutes.
