Onion Pie
 
From:  Gretchen Hess
 
 
3    Cups coarsely chopped mild, sweet onion
3    Tablespoons butter
3     Beaten eggs
3/4    Cup light cream
1 1/2     teaspoon salt
    Dash pepper
1    9” unbaked pie shell
    
    
 
Cook onions in butter.  Line pie shell.  Mix eggs, cream, salt and pour over onions. 
Bake 350° - 35 minutes or until set.
