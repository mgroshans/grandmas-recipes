Chili Sauce
 
From:  Gretchen Hess
 
 
1    Peck tomatoes
6    Onions
3-5    Stalks celery
3    Green peppers
3    Cups white sugar
3    Cups brown sugar
2    Tbsp. salt
1    tsp. cinnamon
1    tsp. cloves
1    tsp. nutmeg
3    Cups vinegar
 
 
Grind vegetables and cook with tomatoes, sugar, vinegar, and spices until desired consistency.
