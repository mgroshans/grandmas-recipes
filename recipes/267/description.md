Spry Frosting
 
From:  Gretchen Hess    
 
 
2    Tablespoons spry
1     Tablespoons butter
5    Tablespoons hot milk
2    Cups of sifted 4x sugar
1/4    teaspoon salt
1    teaspoon vanilla
    
    
 
Cream spry, add a little 4x sugar and beat.  Add part of hot milk, beat again, more sugar then rest of milk and sugar.  Beat until light.
