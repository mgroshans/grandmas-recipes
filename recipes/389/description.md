Chunk Pickles
 
From:  Mrs. Ream
 
7    Pounds cucumbers
3    Pounds brown sugar
3    Pints vinegar
2    teaspoons cinnamon
2    teaspoons cloves
    
    
    
Soak cucumbers over night in salt water.  Boil vinegar, sugar, and spices for a few minutes.  
Add a small piece of alum.  Then add cucumbers and can.
