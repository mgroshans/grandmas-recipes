Rhubarb Pie
 
 
3    Eggs
2 2/3    Tablespoons milk
2    Cups sugar
4    Tablespoons flour
3/4    teaspoon nutmeg
4    Cups rhubarb
1    Tablespon butter
    
 
Cut rhubarb and pour boiling water over and let stand 10 minutes. 
Pour ingredients in pie shell – bake 400° for 50 to 60 minutes.
Make lattice top.
