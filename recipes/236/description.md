Oatmeal Cookies
 
From:  Eunice, 1961
 
 
1    Cup white sugar
1    Stick oleo
1/2    Stick butter
1    Egg
1/2     teaspoon baking soda
1/8    Cup milk, warm
1     Cup oatmeal thru food grinder
1    Cup flour
1/4    teaspoon cinnamon
1/4    teaspoon nutmeg
    Nuts and Raisins thru food grinder
 
 
Bake at 350° 10 to 12 minutes.
