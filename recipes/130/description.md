Date Apple Cake
 
From:  Jean Lentz
 
 
1    Cup chopped dates
1    Cup apple, chopped fine
1    Cup boiling water
1    teaspoon soda
1/2    Cup shortening
1    Cup sugar
1    Egg
1 1/2     Cup flour
1    teaspoon salt
    Vanilla
    
 
Mix dates, apple, water and soda together and let stand while mixing other ingredients.
Mix remaining ingredients and add together.  
Bake at 350° for 40-45 minutes. 
Pan 8x12x2.
 
Topping:
Melt:
4     Tablespoons butter
1/2     Cup brown sugar    
2     Tablespoons milk    
1/2     Cup chopped nuts
1/2     Cup cocoanut 
 
Put on top of cake warm and put under broiler for a few minutes.
