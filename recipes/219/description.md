Poppy Seed Cake
 
From:  C. White
 
 
1    Yellow cake mix
1    Package instant pudding – vanilla
1/2    Cup sherry
1/2    Cup oil
1    Cup sour cream
4    Eggs
1/3    Cup poppy seeds
 
 
Mix together.  Beat at medium speed for 5 minutes.
Bake in greased and floured Bundt pan 45-60 minutes at 350°.
