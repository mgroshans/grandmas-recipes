Clam Dip
 
From:  Khaki
 
 
1    Can minced clams
1    Cup sour cream
1    Tablespoon minced onion
1/2    teaspoon salt
Dash    Pepper
Dash    Garlic powder
1    teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
 
 
Drain clams and combine with sour cream and remaining ingredients.  Chill for 1/2 hour.  Serve with crackers, chips or vegetable dippers.
