Pickled Heart (Brine)
 
From:  Gretchen Hess
 
 
Brine for 1 heart:    
    Beef hearts – steer (young)
4    Cups vinegar
3/4    Cup sugar (scant)
1/2    teaspoon salt 
    Sweet onion, sliced
    Pepper to taste
    Few cloves (whole) and bay leafs can be used
    
 
 
Boil together vinegar, sugar, salt, pepper, cloves and bay leaves.  Cool before pouring on heart.
Simmer heart with fat on until done.
