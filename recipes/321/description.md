Barbecues
 
From:  Dorothy Emerson, 1975
 
 
1    Pound ground beef
1    Tablespoon lard
1    Onion, chopped
1    Green pepper, chopped
2    Tablespoons sugar
2    Tablespoons dry mustard
1    Tablespoon salt
3/4    Cup catsup
    
 
 
Brown ground beef in lard.  Add onion, green pepper, sugar, mustard, salt and catsup.  
Simmer slowly 30 minutes. 
 
Serves 6.
