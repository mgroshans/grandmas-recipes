Krisy's Goulash
 
 
1 1/2    Pounds ground beef
    Onion
    Garlic
    Broth seasoning mix
15 1/2    Ounce spaghetti sauce with mushrooms (or tomato sauce)
1    Pint tomatoes with green pepper
 
 
Brown ground beef.  Add onion and garlic with broth seasoning mix til tender.  
Add spaghetti sauce. 
Add macaroni.
