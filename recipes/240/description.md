Old Fashion Sugar Cookies
 
From:  Minnie
 
 
1 1/2     Cup sugar
1/2    Cup butter
1    Cup sour cream
2 3/4    Cup sifted flour
1/2    teaspoon baking powder
1/2    teaspoon soda
1/2    teaspoon salt
1    teaspoon vanilla
2    Eggs
    
 
 
Chill, then drop.  Press out with fork, sprinkle with sugar.
Bake 350° 8 to 10 minutes.
  (Do Not Overbake)
